About The Festival

Watch it outside events co. is a company owned by Clara Dunn and we believe that film can appeal to audiences of every age in order to prove that, we have traveled to beautiful cities all over the world organizing film festival and the experiences and turn up has been mind-blowing.

This year with the help and support of the Governor of Boston through the departments of Tourism, Sports and Entertainment and the department of Parks and Recreation, we have been given the approval to delight the people of the city of Boston with our amazing films while enjoying the magnificent and natural view of the Boston public garden every evening from 6:00pm up until midnight starting from the 5th to the 8th of August.

The film festival is taking place at the Boston public garden which has a sitting capacity of 1,500 people so to ensure that you are not left out of the fun, we have created a pre-register form to enable you make your registrations for yourself and whoever you intend to bring along and it's absolutely free!
